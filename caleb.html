Caleb Patterson

1998

Caleb was born in the year 1996 in the dry state of Arizona. As the first born Caleb was entitled to certain things... what things you might ask? "Everything" would be his passionate answer. Growing up as the first of five boys Caleb had important decisions to make. To be or not to be? Or more accuratly to be a tyrant or be an example?

Caleb grew up to become one of the sturdiest and strongest men there ever was. Strong willed, determend, hardworking, and gentle Caleb chose to become an inspiring example.

Now one of the top leaders in one of the Nations top solar companies, Caleb continues to help, guide, insprire, and teach others everywhere he goes.
